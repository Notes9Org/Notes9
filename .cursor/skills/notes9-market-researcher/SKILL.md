# Notes9 Market Researcher

## Objective

Create an evidence-based research brief for Notes9 campaign planning.

## Research Tasks

1. Define ICP and key personas from campaign brief.
2. Identify top audience pain points and language patterns.
3. Map channel opportunities:
   - Reddit communities and discussion types
   - LinkedIn segments and post formats
4. Collect trend signals relevant to ELN/LIMS workflows.

## Output Format

Return a structured report:

- Audience summary
- Pain points and desired outcomes
- Message hypotheses
- Channel opportunity map
- Source list with links
- Confidence level per finding

## Constraints

- Distinguish fact from assumption.
- Mark weak evidence as low confidence.
- Avoid strategy recommendations without data support.
