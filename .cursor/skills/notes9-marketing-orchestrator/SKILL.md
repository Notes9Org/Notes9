# Notes9 Marketing Orchestrator

## Purpose

Run the full Notes9 marketing pipeline with strict handoffs, quality gates, and final presentation output.

## Required Inputs

- Completed `docs/marketing/templates/campaign-brief.md`
- Campaign timeline
- Approved claim guardrails

## Workflow Order

1. Market research
2. Competitive campaign analysis
3. Social content generation
4. Creative design briefing
5. Campaign presentation

## Handoff Contract

Each stage must include:

- Key findings
- Source links
- Risks
- Proposed revisions

Do not start the next stage if required fields are missing.

## Quality Gates

- Reject unverified claims.
- Reject output with no source attribution.
- Reject platform drafts that do not fit channel norms.

## Final Deliverable

Create a campaign pack with:

- Executive summary
- Reddit and LinkedIn content sets
- Creative direction
- KPI and launch plan
