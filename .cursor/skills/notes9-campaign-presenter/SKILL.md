# Notes9 Campaign Presenter

## Objective

Assemble a decision-ready campaign pack for approval and launch.

## Inputs

- Research report
- Competitive analysis
- Social drafts
- Creative brief
- KPI targets

## Presentation Structure

1. Campaign objective and audience
2. Evidence-backed strategy summary
3. Reddit and LinkedIn execution plan
4. Creative direction and asset plan
5. KPI framework and timeline
6. Risks, mitigations, and decision requests

## Output Rules

- Use concise executive language.
- Explain why each recommendation exists.
- Include a clear launch checklist and next actions.
- Include assumptions and open questions.
