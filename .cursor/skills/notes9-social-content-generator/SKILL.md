# Notes9 Social Content Generator

## Objective

Generate post-ready drafts for Reddit and LinkedIn with channel-fit messaging.

## Inputs

- Campaign brief
- Market research outputs
- Competitive analysis outputs
- Claim guardrails

## Output Requirements

Provide:

- 3-5 Reddit variants
- 3-5 LinkedIn variants
- CTA options by platform
- Suggested publication sequence

## Channel Rules

### Reddit

- Lead with problem and insight.
- Keep educational and practical tone.
- Use soft CTA and discussion-first framing.

### LinkedIn

- Lead with authority and workflow outcomes.
- Use clear B2B value framing.
- Use stronger CTA tied to demo or conversation.

## Quality Checklist

- No unverifiable claims
- No hype-heavy language
- Clear value proposition in every draft
