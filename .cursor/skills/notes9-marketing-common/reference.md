# Notes9 Marketing Common Reference

This reference is shared across Notes9 marketing skills.

## Core Message Pillars

1. Agentic research workflow support
2. Unified ELN + LIMS operating model
3. Faster, better-structured scientific execution

## Channel Positioning Defaults

- Reddit: insight-first, educational, discussion-friendly
- LinkedIn: authority-led, proof-oriented, business outcome framing

## Claim Safety Rules

- Do not claim validated outcomes without source evidence.
- Do not use clinical or medical efficacy language unless approved.
- Keep comparisons factual and attributable.

## Required Evidence Markers

Every strategic recommendation should include:

- What was observed
- Where it was observed
- Confidence level (high/medium/low)

## Vocabulary Guidance

- Prefer: "workflow acceleration", "research operations", "structured experimentation"
- Avoid: "guaranteed results", "instant transformation", "medical breakthrough"
